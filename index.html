The hotel

The ceremony and reception will be taking advantage of the beautiful Caravelle Hotel and Resort of Myrtle Beach, South Carolina.

Since its inception in 1960, The Caravelle Resort has established itself as a landmark of Myrtle Beach, South Carolina. Located in the famed Golden Mile of Myrtle Beach among the best attractions, shopping, dining and golf, The Caravelle Resort sets itself apart with dedication to service, extraordinary amenities and top notch accommodations. Featuring nine buildings and over 600 units, The Caravelle offers more choices than any Myrtle Beach resort with its variety of efficiencies, suites and condominiums that will suit any size group.

When it came time to think about our honeymoon we knew we wanted to take a trip to Europe. Both of us have traveled outside of the United States but as much as we have traveled we have never been out of the states together. We also felt we should travel somewhere neither of us have been so we could experience the country for the first time together. We combined Beth's love for the arts and wine with Bryan's interests in the ancient ruins and the fermentation process in general to make the decision to go to Italy. It is the birthplace of some of the greats: Michaelangelo, Da Vinci, Rembrandt, and more (but we'll skip those to avoid typos). We'll see where the gladiators fought, visit vineyards in Tuscany, and ride gondolas through the "streets" of Venice. We are looking forward to starting the next chapter of our life together... marriage... with an amazing trip to Italy!
